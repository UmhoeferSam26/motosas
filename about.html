Who We Are

Motosas focuses on practical, evidence-driven tools that help organisations reduce the risk of keyless car theft without adding friction to everyday use.

Our Focus

Keyless entry has changed how drivers interact with vehicles and how organised criminals attack them. Motosas exists to help insurers, fleets and dealers respond with simple, scalable prevention tools.

Built Around Real World Use

Our starting point is always the driver: where keys are stored, how routines actually work in busy households, and how to support simple habits that reduce exposure to relay attacks.

Motosas pouches are designed to be clear, durable and easy to explain at point of sale or within policy documentation. They are intended to sit alongside, not replace, other security measures such as tracking, immobilisers and driveway security.

Partnership Led Approach

We work with B2B partners on volumes, branding and deployment. That can include co-branded pouches, insertion into welcome packs, targeted distribution for specific portfolios or use as part of claims prevention campaigns.

Our aim is to provide a tool that is straightforward for frontline teams to describe and simple for customers to use correctly day after day.

Technology & Positioning

Motosas pouches are based on Faraday style RF shielding, a principle that has been used for decades in sectors such as defence and aerospace.

Proven Principles

Conductive layers help reduce or block RF signals across key frequency bands commonly used by keyless vehicle systems when the pouch is used correctly.

Testing Friendly

Independent testing can be aligned to recognised RF attenuation methods to check performance for specific key and vehicle combinations.

Clear Risk Language

We describe Motosas as a prevention tool that supports wider risk reduction strategies, rather than as a guarantee that a vehicle cannot be stolen.
