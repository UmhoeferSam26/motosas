Branding & Artwork Process

We keep the artwork process simple so your marketing or design teams can move quickly.

1

Choose Format & Colourway

Select pouch size (single key, dual key, household), base colour and any contrast stitching or trim options. We can suggest combinations that work well with your brand palette.

2

Supply Logos & Guidelines

Provide vector artwork (SVG, AI or high-res PNG) along with any brand-guideline notes such as clear-space rules, colour usage and minimum sizes.

3

Approve Digital Mock-ups

We provide clear digital mock-ups for sign-off before production, showing front, rear and interior positioning for logos, text and any QR codes or URLs.
