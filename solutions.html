Our Solutions for Your Business

Motosas offers tailored Faraday pouch solutions for businesses seeking to reduce keyless car theft risk for their customers and fleet vehicles.

Brand Awareness for All Businesses

Every Motosas pouch is an opportunity to reinforce your brand while providing genuine value to your customers. By giving branded Faraday pouches to customers, you demonstrate commitment to their safety and security.

Build Trust Through Protection

When customers receive a high-quality Faraday pouch bearing your company logo, they see tangible evidence of your commitment to their wellbeing. This physical reminder sits in their home, reinforcing your brand every time they secure their car keys.

Unlike digital communications that can be ignored or forgotten, a branded Faraday pouch provides lasting value that customers will use daily. This creates positive brand associations and strengthens customer loyalty.

Cost-Effective Marketing

Branded Faraday pouches offer exceptional return on marketing investment. They serve both a practical security function and a continuous brand presence in customers' homes and vehicles.

Whether you're a small local business or a national chain, customised Faraday pouches can be produced at scale with your branding, colours, and messaging. They work as welcome gifts, loyalty rewards, or value-added items that differentiate your service.

Industry-Specific Solutions

Motosas has developed targeted solutions for key automotive and insurance sectors, each designed around specific operational requirements and customer touchpoints.

Automotive Leasing

Car Leasing Companies

Leasing companies face unique challenges with keyless vehicle security. Your customers drive high-value vehicles that are prime targets for relay attacks, yet they may not feel the same ownership responsibility as traditional car buyers.

Our Solution: Include a branded Faraday pouch with every lease agreement. This demonstrates your commitment to asset protection while educating customers about relay attack risks. The pouch can include your emergency contact details and security guidelines specific to the leased vehicle.

Benefits: Reduce theft claims, demonstrate duty of care, enhance customer onboarding experience, and provide a physical brand presence throughout the lease period.

Insurance Industry

Insurance Companies

Motor insurers are increasingly concerned about keyless car theft claims. Offering Faraday pouches demonstrates proactive risk management and can form part of your underwriting strategy for keyless vehicles.

Our Solution: Distribute branded Faraday pouches to policyholders with keyless entry vehicles, particularly in high-risk postcodes. Include QR codes linking to your claims portal or security advice pages. This can be offered as a policy benefit or incentive for higher-value vehicle covers.

Benefits: Reduce theft claim frequency, demonstrate loss prevention leadership, differentiate policy offerings, and provide documentation of reasonable risk mitigation for underwriting purposes.

Rental Services

Car Rental Companies

Rental companies manage large fleets of high-value keyless vehicles that are constantly changing hands. Relay attacks can occur during rental periods or when vehicles are parked at rental locations.

Our Solution: Provide Faraday pouches with each rental, included in the handover pack. Clear instructions educate temporary users about relay attacks and proper key storage. The pouch can include your 24-hour contact number and vehicle return information.

Benefits: Protect fleet assets, reduce insurance premiums through demonstrated security measures, educate customers about vehicle security, and provide peace of mind during rental periods.

Pre-Owned Market

Second-Hand Dealerships

Second-hand dealerships selling keyless vehicles can use Faraday pouches as a value-added differentiator. Many used car buyers may be unaware of relay attack risks associated with their new purchase.

Our Solution: Include a branded Faraday pouch with every keyless vehicle sale as part of your handover pack. This demonstrates your commitment to customer safety and adds perceived value to the purchase. The pouch can include your warranty information and service contact details.

Benefits: Enhance customer satisfaction, differentiate from competitors, demonstrate professional standards, create lasting brand presence, and provide an additional sales talking point.

Direct-to-Consumer Fulfilment

Seamless Distribution

Rather than managing inventory and distribution yourself, Motosas can handle the entire fulfilment process. You provide the customer list, we produce the branded pouches and ship directly to individual customers on your behalf.

This solution is ideal for insurance companies wanting to reach policyholders, leasing companies distributing to active lessees, or any business seeking to provide security products to existing customers without logistics overhead.

Data Protection Compliance

Our ICO registration means we handle customer data responsibly and in full compliance with UK GDPR requirements. We maintain secure processes for receiving, processing, and protecting customer information used for fulfilment purposes.
